About Me

Hello! I am Bobby Lindsey, a licensed ham radio operator with the call sign KY4HAM. I am based in Louisville, KY, USA, and I have been involved in amateur radio since August 2023. After thinking about getting my amateur radio license for years, I decided to make the most of my time rocking my daughter to sleep and began studying using Ham Radio Prep. After getting my Technician license, I decided to keep going and received my General license. I hope to one day upgrade to an Extra license.

My primary interests in ham radio include HF, VHF/UHF, and DMR. I mainly spend time on Brandmeister Talkgroup 91 and scanning the band on 20 meters. Recently, I've been getting back into POTA. I have setup a mobile station in my car and began activating parks. I mainly operate voice modes but I may pick FT8 up again sometime soon.

When I'm not operating my station, I enjoy spending time with my two daughters. I am also in the very long and slow process of converting a former school bus into a camper. I am a stage 4 colorectal cancer survivor. I work as a Software Engineer at an innovation lab called FirstBuild.
